BUTTERFLY PARK

Butterfly Garden or Butterfly Park is a butterfly conservatory in Pune. It has several enclosures for butterflies and a display room with pictures of different butterfly species. It also has many informative posters throughout the walking track of the garden. There is a sound and light show on butterflies on weekends which is highly recommended.
